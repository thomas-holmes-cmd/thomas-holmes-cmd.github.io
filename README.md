<h3 class = hobby>Music </h3>
<p>
I began playing music from the age of 7 where I first got lessons on the fiddle with my two siblings.I currently play the fiddle and accordion. I got to perform at many 
festivals such as the Feakle festival and Fleadh Ceoil na hÉireann. Music has allowed me to meet many unique people from many walks of life. It has also allowed me to take 
part in fundraising for many charitable causes such as my contribution in the In Tune for Life Orchestra and performing in my local parish fundraising for the community. It 
is an integral part of my life and I am glad I have been comitted to it for the past 12 years.
</p>

<h3 class = "hoobby"> Gym </h3>
<p>I started going to the gym Febuary 2022. Since then I have trying to take better care of my health and improving my physical streingth to be able to live a longer and better life.
My friends and I would go nearly every weekend during the Summer holiday and gave us something better to be doing than going to the pub every week. I gained a lot of love going to 
the gym and feeling better every time I go. My favourite parts are when you hit a specific milestone in a lift or you notice an improvement phisically about yourself that proves how 
the time and effort pays off in the end. I don't think I will ever stop going to the gym and I hope that I will be able to constantly improve myself.
